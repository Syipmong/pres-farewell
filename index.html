Farewell to the Outgoing President of Nigeria

May 29th 2015 - May 29th 2023

Muhammadu Buhari

Dear President Muhammadu Buhari, As your tenure comes to an end, we wanted to take a moment to express our heartfelt gratitude for your dedicated service to the nation. Over the past eight years, you have once again graced Nigeria with your leadership, bringing with you a wealth of experience from your previous term as the country's military head of state from 1983 to 1985. Your unwavering commitment and tireless efforts have been instrumental in shaping Nigeria's path towards progress and development. From the moment you assumed office, you faced the challenges head-on and worked tirelessly to address them. Under your guidance, our nation has witnessed remarkable achievements in various sectors, and your determination to tackle corruption and enhance transparency has set an example for future leaders. As a retired Nigerian army major general, you have always been known for your strong leadership and commitment to serving the people. The term "Buharism" is attributed to the authoritative policies of your military regime, which exemplified discipline, integrity, and a no-nonsense approach. Throughout your tenure, you have remained steadfast in your dedication to promoting peace, stability, and unity among Nigerians. You have championed initiatives to strengthen the economy, improve infrastructure, and enhance the welfare of the people. Your commitment to empowering the youth, promoting gender equality, and fostering an inclusive society has had a profound impact on our nation's progress. Your leadership, both past and present, has left an indelible mark on the hearts and minds of the Nigerian people. Your resilience, courage, and unwavering determination in the face of challenges have earned you the respect and admiration of the nation. Your selfless service to Nigeria will be remembered for generations to come. As you embark on a new chapter in your life, we extend our heartfelt appreciation for your service to our nation. We wish you continued success, good health, and happiness. May the experiences and lessons gained during your tenure guide you towards new achievements and endeavors. Thank you, President Muhammadu Buhari, for your exceptional leadership, your devotion to Nigeria, and the positive impact you have made. Farewell, and may your future be filled with abundant blessings. With warm regards, The People of Nigeria
